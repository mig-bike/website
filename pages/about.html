How do we save / load our data?
Make sure you have something to save, and then press the IMPORT/EXPORT button on the top of the screen. Then, press the button that corresponds to the item you want to save / load!
How do we create / edit flashcard decks?
Go to the home screen, then press the big GO button under flashcards. You can create a deck from that menu! After that, press on the flashcard icon that pops up (you may have to reload) and then press the EDIT button on the screen that pops up.
What is the red text under my decks?
That is the spaced repetition timer! It will show you the time that you should wait if you wanted to study with spaced repetition, which is a research based method in order to study more effectively.
